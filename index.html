Facts About Bacillus Anthracis!

1) Bacillus Anthracis can take on many forms!

Bacillus Anthracis when in the human body can develop in the skin, intestines, or in the respiration system.

2) A human can be infected with Bacillus Anthracis Through Animals!

If a human handles infected animals, meat products, or animal products, they could be infected with anthrax.

In October of 2001, shortly after 9/11, it was used by Al-Qaeda for bioterror. Bacillus Anthracis spores were in mail and sent thoughout the U.S.. 22 people were diagnosed with Anthrax and 5 people died.
